Workshops & Special Events




Join Mary Reilly, Leslie Freyberg, & Bob Gilbo for a unique, in-person Iyengar yoga retreat in the serene beauty of Northern Michigan this September...

Weekend Retreat | September 12-14, 2025
The Māhabhūtas Within and Without

Exploring the five primary elements—earth, water, fire, air, & space—within the body and without fosters a sense of interconnectedness between self, other, and environment. Through āsana, prāṇāyāma, Sanskrit chanting, and yoga philosophy, we’ll consider how we live in the elements and the elements live in us. Awakening this awareness is a key to experiencing BKS Iyengar’s statement that “Yoga replenishes the reservoirs of hope and optimism.”

Optional Pre-Retreat Add-on | September 11, 2025
Elemental Sanskrit | Doing, Hearing, Seeing

Newcomers and those with some Sanskrit background alike: develop more ease and confidence in your vocabulary and pronunciation of essential Sanskrit words, āsana names, and philosophical terms. Engage all your senses in the yoga of sound: experience the vibrations of some simple Sanskrit chants and explore how their deep resonance can enhance your practice.

6 months of Iyengar yoga experience recommended for āsana sessions
